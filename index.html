LDL & VLDL Cholesterol Calculator

Calculate your LDL ("bad cholesterol") and VLDL levels using the Friedewald formula

How to Use This Calculator

  1. Enter your Total Cholesterol value from your blood test (in mg/dL)
  2. Enter your HDL Cholesterol ("good cholesterol") value (in mg/dL)
  3. Enter your Triglycerides value (in mg/dL)
  4. Click "Calculate" to see your LDL and VLDL cholesterol levels
Note: This calculator uses the Friedewald formula. For accurate results, fasting for 9-12 hours before your blood test is recommended.
